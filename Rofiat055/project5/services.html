  • Website Hosting

    Web hosting is a service that allows businesses and individuals to publish their websites or web pages on the Internet. A web host, also known as a web hosting service provider, is a company that offers the technologies and services required for a website or webpage to be viewed on the Internet. Websites are hosted, or saved, on specialized computers known as servers. When Internet users want to view your website, they simply enter your website address or domain into their browser.
