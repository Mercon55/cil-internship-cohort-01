    The concepts of OOP

    Encapsulation
    Encapsulation is the process of wrapping up of data (properties) and behaviour (methods) of an object into a single unit.
    Polymorphism
    'Poly' means 'many' and 'morph' means 'forms'. So polymorphism means the ability of an object to change its form.
    Inheritance
    Inheritance describes the parent child relationship between two classes. A class can get some of its characteristics from a parent class.
    Abstraction
    Abstraction is the ability to make a class abstract. To be abstract means to be a concept that does not have a specific or concrete existence
